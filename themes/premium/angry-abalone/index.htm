Angry abalone is minimalistic theme. The sidebar is removed. It allows the reader's attention to fall solely on the content. Perfect for users who wants a simple theme with no bells and whistles. It comes in a variety of colors: white, dark, light blue. We also included 2 variations that uses simple patterns for the background.

Being a premium theme, you'll receive regular updates and support. Installation is ridiculously easy. Using the theme does not affect your existing themes or blog posts.
